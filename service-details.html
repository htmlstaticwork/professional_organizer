Specialized Hoarding Support

Hoarding is a complex situation that requires more than just cleaning. It requires understanding, patience, and a psych-informed approach to decluttering.

What we provide

Our process is divided into manageable steps to ensure the client remains comfortable and in control. We focus on the person, not just the items.

  • Assessment & Trust Building

    Initial meeting to understand the situation and build a foundation of safety.

  • Strategic Sort & Purge

    Assisting in the decision-making process with gentle guidance.

  • Home System Setup

    Re-organizing the recovered space for better functionality.

Who is this for?

This service is designed for individuals struggling with chronic disorganization, hoarding disorder, or families assisting a loved one in a recovery process. We also collaborate with therapists and social workers.
