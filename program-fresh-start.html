4-WEEK INTENSIVE

The Fresh Start

An intensive, short-term program focused on purging and initial organization of the most used living spaces. This is perfect for those who are overwhelmed and need immediate, visible results.

Weekly Roadmap

Week 1: The Primary Sanctuary

We start in the primary bedroom and closet. By clearing your resting area first, we ensure you have a peaceful 'home base' to recharge in throughout the program.

Week 2: Living Area Restoration

Clearing the main living spaces where life happens. We focus on surface-level organization and functional flow to reduce immediate visual stress.

Week 3: The Functional Kitchen

Purging expired items and organizing high-use cabinets. We ensure you have the space to prepare healthy meals without the stress of clutter.

Week 4: The Path Forward

Final review, donation removal, and setting up basic maintenance routines to ensure your 'fresh start' becomes a permanent reality.
