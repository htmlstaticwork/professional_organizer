SPECIALIZED SUPPORT

Hoarding Support

Recovery from hoarding is a deeply personal journey. Our psych-informed approach prioritizes the individual's safety, dignity, and pace above all else.

The Support Framework

Empathetic Assessment

We don't just count boxes. We listen to your story, understand your triggers, and build a foundation of trust before a single item is moved.

Collaborative Sorting

Using proven behavioral techniques, we help you navigate the 'decision paralysis' often associated with hoarding, ensuring you retain absolute agency over your home.

Intelligent Disposal & Resource Management

We coordinate confidential removals, hazardous waste disposal, and donation logistics, removing the physical burden of the transition from your shoulders.

Therapeutic Collaboration

We frequently collaborate with therapists and healthcare providers to ensure our organizing efforts align with your broader mental wellness goals. We believe environmental recovery is a vital pillar of clinical success.
