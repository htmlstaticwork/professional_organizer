The Recovery Journey

Our flagship 3-month program is specifically designed for individuals and families dealing with significant clutter or hoarding challenges.

How it works

Month 1: Establishing Focus

Building the emotional toolkit required to let go. We start in small, low-stress areas to build confidence and trust.

Month 2: Strategic Restoration

Hands-on sorting and removal phases. We clear the living areas, kitchen, and bathroom to ensure the fundamental functions of the home are restored.

Month 3: Sustainable Systems

Setting up permanent organizational systems that are easy to maintain. Creating a "Clarity Routine" for the daily life.
