A flexible monthly maintenance and coaching program for those who have already transformed their space but want professional accountability to keep it that way forever.

One 2-hour session per month where we walk through your home, identify emerging clutter, and recalibrate your organizational systems to your current lifestyle.

Digital Coaching & Accountability

Weekly check-ins via our secure client portal. You send pictures, we send feedback and adjustments to keep you motivated between sessions.
